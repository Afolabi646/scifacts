About Us

Welcome to SciFacts, a platform dedicated to exploring the fascinating intersection of science and Islam. Our mission is to showcase the rich scientific heritage of Islamic civilization and to highlight the remarkable connections between modern scientific discoveries and the teachings of the Quran.

Our Founder

Ustaz Abubakre Akanji is the founder and driving force behind SciFacts. With a passion for science, knowledge, Islam and education, Ustaz Abubakre Akanji aims to inspire a new generation of Muslims to explore the natural world and to see the beauty and wonder of creation. Ustaz Abubakre Akanji holds a degree in Arabic from Arabic and Islamic Training Centre, Agege, Lagos, Nigeria and also at the Azhar University Cairo, Egypt. and has years of experience in Arabic Learning.
